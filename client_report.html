WDD 130 Client Project Report

Client

As a client for this project, I was able to ask someone to create a project for me to share my crochet projects. This helped me to understand the perspective that clients sharing their projects might have. I had to decide what content I wanted to have on this page which was fairly simple once I decided what I wanted my project to be. I considered what content similar websites had and decided to model mine after them. As the client, I was able to communicate what I wanted my finished website to look like to my lead developer, which we accomplished through email. The website looked to be coming along how I asked fir it, which is great. If I were to hire a team to do a project for me in the future, I would most likely attempt to communicate more often throughout the project.

General Experience

This project was very helpful to know how to handle any potential projects in the future. The most difficult part of this assignment was having to work with four seperate groups, with people I have not worked with before. This led to some confusion between groups, but we were able to work through it. I enjoyed seeing the website come together as multiple people contributed to it. If I were to do this project again, I would try to meet with group members in person to clarify any confusion for each project. I think I do feel more confident working on real-life projects after this because I was able to gain experience and perspective on what I would do for future projects.
